# Study Guide 03 — Fundamentals First

## Rep
- Psalm 119:11
- James 1:22
- Matthew 7:24

## One Big Idea
The Word becomes your stance. Fundamentals create stability under pressure.

## Discuss
1) What do you return to when life gets stressful?
2) What “stance” do you want to build?
3) Why is repeatability more important than intensity?

## Drill
Build your 3–3–1 stance: 3 verses, 3 cues, 1 daily obedience step.

## Film Room
Which cue do you need most this week—and why?

## Closing
“God, make Your Word my stance when life speeds up.”
