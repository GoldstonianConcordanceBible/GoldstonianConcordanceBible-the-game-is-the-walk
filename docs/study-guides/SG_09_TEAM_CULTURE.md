# Study Guide 09 — Team Culture

## Rep
- Joshua 24:15
- Hebrews 10:24
- 1 Corinthians 15:33

## One Big Idea
Standards make the program. Culture disciples faster than speeches.

## Discuss
1) What behaviors are normal around you?
2) What do you tolerate that you shouldn’t?
3) What standard do you want to raise?

## Drill
S.T.A.N.D.: Speak, Train, Apply, Normalize, Defend one standard.

## Film Room
What standard needs to be clarified in your life?

## Closing
“God, help us hold the standard with love and consistency.”
