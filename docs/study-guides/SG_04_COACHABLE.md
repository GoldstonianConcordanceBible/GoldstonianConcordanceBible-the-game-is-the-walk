# Study Guide 04 — Coachable

## Rep
- Proverbs 3:11–12
- Hebrews 12:6
- Proverbs 27:6

## One Big Idea
Correction is coaching—information that helps you grow.

## Discuss
1) How do you usually respond to correction?
2) What’s the difference between correction and rejection?
3) Who corrects you with love?

## Drill
Use C.A.G.E.: Calm, Ask, Give thanks, Execute one rep.

## Film Room
What is one adjustment you need right now?

## Closing
“God, make me humble, teachable, and quick to adjust.”
