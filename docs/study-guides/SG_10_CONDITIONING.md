# Study Guide 10 — Conditioning

## Rep
- Hebrews 12:1
- 1 Timothy 4:7–8

## One Big Idea
Endurance is trained. Strength comes from repetition.

## Discuss
1) Where do you fade when things go long?
2) What habit helps you recover?
3) What does “finish the rep” mean for you?

## Drill
Spiritual Conditioning Circuit (12 min): scripture endurance, stillness, obedience carry, gratitude.

## Film Room
