# 12-Week Team Plan — The Game is the Walk

This is a ready-to-run season plan for:
- Baseball/softball teams
- Families
- Small groups

Weekly rhythm:
- Day 1 (10–15 min): Rep + Drill
- Day 2 (5–10 min): Film Room
- Day 3 (2–5 min): Game Day Walk
- Optional: Standard reminder (2 min)

---

## Week 1 — The Walk Is the Season
**Rep:** Psalm 119:105; Micah 6:8  
**Drill:** 7-day rep streak (read + next-step obedience daily)  
**Film Room:** What’s my next step this week?  
**Game Day:** Take one next step within 24 hours  
**Standard:** “We show up.”

## Week 2 — Show Up
**Rep:** Galatians 6:9  
**Drill:** Minimum Reps Rule (6 minutes daily)  
**Film Room:** What steals my reps most often?  
**Game Day:** Show up even tired  
**Standard:** “We are dependable.”

## Week 3 — Fundamentals First
**Rep:** James 1:22; Psalm 119:11  
**Drill:** 3–3–1 stance (3 verses, 3 cues, 1 daily do)  
**Film Room:** Which cue do I need most?  
**Game Day:** Reset to stance under stress  
**Standard:** “We return to fundamentals.”

## Week 4 — Coachable
**Rep:** Hebrews 12:6; Proverbs 3:11–12  
**Drill:** C.A.G.E. (Calm, Ask, Give thanks, Execute a rep)  
**Film Room:** One adjustment I need now  
**Game Day:** Receive correction without defense  
**Standard:** “We honor coaching.”

## Week 5 — The Secret Place
**Rep:** Matthew 6:6  
**Drill:** 10-minute cage time (same time, same place)  
**Film Room:** Who am I becoming in secret?  
**Game Day:** Protect your private reps  
**Standard:** “We train when no one is watching.”

## Week 6 — Film Room
**Rep:** Psalm 139:23–24; Lamentations 3:40  
**Drill:** Weekly review (one win, one drift, one adjustment, one rep)  
**Film Room:** What pattern repeats under stress?  
**Game Day:** Make one clean adjustment  
**Standard:** “We tell the truth.”

## Week 7 — Strikeouts
**Rep:** 1 John 1:9; Proverbs 24:16  
**Drill:** R.E.P. reset (Recognize, Examine, Practice)  
**Film Room:** What truth replaces accusation?  
**Game Day:** Fail forward—don’t quit  
**Standard:** “We recover with maturity.”

## Week 8 — Plate Discipline
**Rep:** Proverbs 4:23; James 1:19  
**Drill:** T.W.L. filter (True, Wise, Loving)  
**Film Room:** What pitch do I need to let pass?  
**Game Day:** Take the pitch (don’t react)  
**Standard:** “We don’t chase distractions.”

## Week 9 — Team Culture
**Rep:** Hebrews 10:24; Joshua 24:15  
**Drill:** S.T.A.N.D. one standard (Speak, Train, Apply, Normalize, Defend)  
**Film Room:** What do we tolerate that we must train?  
**Game Day:** Hold the line with love  
**Standard:** “Standards make the program.”

## Week 10 — Conditioning
**Rep:** Hebrews 12:1; 1 Timothy 4:7–8  
**Drill:** Conditioning circuit (scripture endurance, stillness, obedience carry, gratitude)  
**Film Room:** Where do I fade when it’s long?  
**Game Day:** Finish the rep  
**Standard:** “We build endurance.”

## Week 11 — Game Day Pressure
**Rep:** James 1:2–4; 1 Peter 4:12  
**Drill:** Game Day Routine (cue reset → next step → post-review)  
**Film Room:** What held? What slipped?  
**Game Day:** Trust your training  
**Standard:** “Pressure reveals preparation.”

## Week 12 — Legacy
**Rep:** Psalm 78:4; 2 Timothy 2:2  
**Drill:** 3–1–1 plan (3 people, 1 habit, 1 sentence)  
**Film Room:** If someone copied my walk, where would it lead them?  
**Game Day:** Teach one person one habit  
**Standard:** “We pass the Walk forward.”
